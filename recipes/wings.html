Buffalo Wings

Description

I hope you guys like spicy food because today we are making Buffalo Wings!

This is a simple recipe that is a great addition to any party,

The recipe is pretty flexible so if you need to add less or more of something it isn't a big deal.

Here's a little history on the Buffalo Wing.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Drumsticks
  • Seasoning
  • Hot Sauce

Steps

  1. If you have an Airfryer or a traditional fryer make sure it is heated up
  2. Fry drumsticks per directions
  3. Place drumsticks in a sealable bag once finished cooking
  4. Pour in as much seasoning as you would like
  5. Pour hot sauce in bag
  6. Shake vigorously till all wings are generously covered
  7. Place on plate and add celery or ranch to the side
