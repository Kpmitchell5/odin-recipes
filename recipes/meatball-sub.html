Meatball Sub

Description

This along with the other recipes in the list are great foods for parties, they are simple and easy to make. Hopefully since you found your way to this page you are ready to make some delicious Meatball Subs!

Ingredients

  • Meatballs
  • Red sauce
  • Sub buns
  • Cheese Slices
  • (Optional)Toppings to put on the sub

Steps

  1. Thoroughly cook your meatballs
  2. Slice sub bun
  3. Place meatballs in sub buns
  4. Cover meatballs with red sauce
  5. If adding additional toppings now is then time
  6. Place slices of cheese on top
  7. Place sub on baking sheet and bake until cheese is melted
  8. Enjoy
