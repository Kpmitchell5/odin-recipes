Chicken and Broccoli

Description

This is a staple with people trying to eat healthier, go to the gym or just enjoy chicken and broccoli. I've long been a fan of the two and i'm always in a hurry so this is a good recipe for someone who just needs to cook something quick.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Seasoning for the chicken
  • Broccoli
  • Seasoning or Cheese for Broccoli
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Take the Chicken Breasts and season to desired amount
  2. Pour a decent amount of oil and cover entire bottom of saute pan
  3. Place chicken in saute pan
  4. Saute till cooked through
  5. Season the broccoli to the desired amount
  6. In another pan or the same one saute the broccoli
  7. Place all food on plate
